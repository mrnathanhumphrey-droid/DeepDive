RULES FOR SOCIAL MEDIA CITATIONS:
1. Social media posts are ONLY valid sources for BREAKING mode topics — never for current, recent, or general.
2. Always cross-reference: a social media post alone is not sufficient. It must be corroborated by at least one wire service report or official government statement within 24 hours.
3. Cite the account name, platform, date/time, and exact quote or paraphrase.
4. Flag any social media-only claim as "social_media_unconfirmed" in your citation block until wire service confirmation is found.
5. Do NOT cite unofficial accounts, anonymous accounts, or unverified sources regardless of follower count.
